About me

10 years in excellency.

I'm a college student based in Semarang, Indonesia. I started my journey into graphic design and web design since 2010. My first site was up in 2011 on blogspot, 000webhost and tumblr. Joined webdesign extracurricular at my high school and became a leader in 2011. Later I was going to Universitas Sebelas Maret, majoring in Informatics. Joined Himaster from 2014, later from PDD (Publication and Design) to PSDM (Human Resources). In 2018 I changed to Dian Nuswantoro University while still in the same major.

Some of my projects are banner/poster design for annual event, personal projects, and freelancing. Programs I used are vary. Joomla, CorelDraw, Photoscape, PaintTool SAI, and Clip Studio Paint.
